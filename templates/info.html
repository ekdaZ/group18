It's time for you

to FOCUS

This is a web-based program which helps you track how you spend your day, so that YOU can seize it as well as possible!

Why choose Focal?

Don't Forget...

Focal remembers all of your activities and will make sure you will not forget it so make sure not to ignore incoming notifications! The activities will be placed in weekly timetable for you to easily see what you have for the day. By the end of the week it will return an overview of what you did for the whole week.

Time is Ticking...

Whenever an activity starts, the timer will begin the countdown! Make sure you stay focused within that period of time or else... Once the timer is finished, it will play a sound to indicate that time is up. Not to mention there will be a win streak for you; if completed, you will be rewarded generously!
